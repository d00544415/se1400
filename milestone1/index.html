Website Summary

Una página de comida divertida, fresca e innovadora.

What content will the site contain?

Photos and information about our food, mission, vision, and information about new events, products, location, and contact details.

Where will you get all of the content for your site (ie. images & text)?

The main idea is to create our own content in terms of photos, videos, and general information.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

We want you to focus on the diversity, quality, and presentation of our products.

Why should a site be built for this?

Because it is the most economical, efficient, and popular way to distribute products and market them.

What would you like users to do at your site?

I would like our users to be able to find out about our products and recipes, place orders online, make payments, and give suggestions.

What is the goal of the site?

Attract as many customers as possible to our business with low-cost advertising.
